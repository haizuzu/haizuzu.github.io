Serving Size Preparation Time
2 people 1 hour 20 minutes

Ingredients for Curry sauce

  • 200g onion, finely diced
  • 1/2 celery stick, finely diced
  • 100g apple (quarter of a large apple), peeled and grated
  • 20g beef tallow/butter
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp grated garlic
  • 1tbsp curry powder
  • 600 ml water
  • 1 small(ish) potato, cubed
  • 2 cubes curry sauce mix

Preparation of Curry Sauce

Caramelize onion with a pinch of salt.
Add garlic, ginger, and apple, and evaporate the water.
Add curry powder, mix on low heat.
Add water and potatos, skim off gunk, and let boil for 15 minutes.
Turn off heat, add 2 block of curry sauce mix, stir in until melted.
Leave covered.

Ingredients for Katsu

  • 2 slices katsu loin
  • Breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 4tbsp cake flour

Preparation of Katsu

Stab katsu with a fork all over.
Season with salt and pepper on both sides.
Mix egg and water in a bowl. Add flour and mix.
Cover katsu in the batter, and then breadcrumbs.
Fry katsu individually in 170Â°C oil for 5 minutes, flipping occasionally.
Remove excess breadcrumbs, and leave katsu to cool on a drying rack.
Slice the katsu, and serve on top of a plate of rice and curry sauce.
